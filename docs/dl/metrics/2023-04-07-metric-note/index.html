    Entropy

    An information content or uncertainty measurements. The higher the entropy, the harder to predict value of an random variable from a given distribution.

    The entropy of a discrete random variable XXX with distribution ppp consisting of KKK states is:

    H(X)≜−∑k=1Kp(X=k)=−EX[log⁡p(X)] \begin{aligned} H(X) &\triangleq -\sum^K_{k=1}{p(X=k)}\\ &=-\mathbb{E}_X[\log{p(X)}] \end{aligned} H(X)​≜−k=1∑K​p(X=k)=−EX​[logp(X)]​
